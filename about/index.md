Hello there! I'm Dishant Langayan, from a small, but wonderfull Canadian city - Ottawa. I've created this blog to share some of my experiences, stories, and all the techie stuff I'm passionate about. Working at [Solace](https://solace.com) for 9+ year, I have worn many hats. I started off as a consultant, working with several of our customers in investment banks, stock exchanges, smart city projects, to leading a new product introduction program for our SaaS product, and then moving on to sales enablement and managing training for customers, partner, and employees. But the developer inside of me still likes to write code time-to-time.

Over my time at Solace, I have worked on many Event Driven Architectures (EDA) and digital transformation projects, and through this blog I will share some of my real-world experiences & knowledge on EDAs, design patterns, and how enterprises are modernizing and going through digital transformation by becoming event driven and real time. 

When I'm not in front of my computer, I love to travel and explore the world, experience it's diverse culture & food, and capture some moments on my camera.

I have been travelling since I was born...lived in 9 countries, and travelled to over 50 cities. According to my [TripAdvisor travel map](https://www.tripadvisor.ca/TravelMap-a_uid.4D9E15C7214416E91BF484EFD77D3B4B) I have see 14% of the world! So now I just need to tackle the remaining 86% :)

Hope you enjoy reading this blog!
